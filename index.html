What is a vehicle sourcing broker?

We work for you — not a dealership.

Unlike a dealership that sells from its own lot, Clearev Consultants is an independent broker. We have no inventory, no sales quotas, and no manufacturer incentives. Our only job is to find your vehicle at the best possible price — from any dealer, auction, or private seller.

That means you get access to a far wider selection than any single dealership can offer, with someone negotiating on your behalf — not against you. We are paid a flat fee by you, so our interests are always aligned with yours.

Questions

Questions we get
before people book.

Can I just negotiate myself? +

You can. The dealer's finance manager has done this thousands of times this year. You've done it twice. Experience is the advantage — and right now they have it.

What if I don't save more than your fee? +

That has not happened. But if your situation is unusual, we will discuss it on your free strategy call before you commit to anything.

How do I know you're not connected to dealerships? +

We are paid only by you. No dealer referral fees. No manufacturer incentives. No conflicts of interest.

How long does the process take? +

Most deals close within 5 to 7 business days from your intake call, depending on vehicle availability.

Do you work on used cars too? +

Yes. Used car sourcing is available under the Full Concierge tier given the additional complexity of locating and vetting pre-owned inventory.
